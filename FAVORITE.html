Exotic Foods

FRIED BAKI

Garlic Butter Fried Frog Legs are deep-fried to golden perfection and smothered in a flavorful sauce for the ultimate appetizer or main dish. Delicious on their own or with steamed rice!.

FRIED GRASSHOPPER

Fried grasshoppers are a crunchy, tasty snack made by deep-frying grasshoppers and seasoning them with various spices.

FRIED ULOD

Fried larvae are a crunchy, nutty-flavored food that can be made from a variety of insect larvae.

FRIED BFF

Fried snake can be described as chewy and having a mild flavor, similar to frog legs

FRIED TARANTULA

Fried tarantulas are a regional delicacy in Cambodia that are crispy on the outside and soft on the inside. Some say they taste like crab or shrimp, while others describe the flavor as bland or a cross between chicken and cod.
